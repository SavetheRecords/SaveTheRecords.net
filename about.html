About Rambone Records

Rambone Records is an independent, specialized vinyl distributor cataloging releases of all eras. Our collection represents decades of musical history with a focus on high-fidelity pressings and pristine, collectible conditions.

We handle each shipping transaction personally to safeguard our records. Clean packaging practices ensure that everything we sell arrives at your door matching the description details listed.

Direct Grading Standards

We use the standard Goldmine grading framework to classify physical components. Each listing is visually analyzed and, where necessary, spot-tested to supply an accurate description profile.
